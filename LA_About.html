    Los Angeles Home Remodeling You Can Trust

    New Dawn Construction has spent over 15 years helping homeowners across Los Angeles County transform their living spaces. From full kitchen overhauls in Burbank and Glendale to spa-inspired bathroom renovations in Pasadena and Sherman Oaks, every project reflects our commitment to quality craftsmanship and transparent communication. We hold California Contractor License #1087088 and carry comprehensive liability and workers' compensation coverage so you can start your remodel with complete peace of mind.

    Our design-build approach means you work with one team from concept through completion. We handle architectural plans, city permits, material sourcing, and construction under a single contract. That streamlined process eliminates the finger-pointing and scheduling conflicts that plague projects split among separate designers, architects, and builders. Homeowners consistently tell us the clarity of our fixed-price proposals and weekly progress updates removed the stress they feared most about remodeling.

    Whether you need a revenue-generating accessory dwelling unit, a sun-filled room addition for a growing family, or custom built-in cabinetry that maximizes every square foot, we bring the same meticulous attention to detail. Our portfolio includes more than 1,000 completed projects spanning kitchen remodeling, bathroom renovation, ADU construction, room additions, and custom carpentry throughout communities like Studio City, La Cañada Flintridge, Santa Clarita, Beverly Hills, and Santa Monica.

    Ready to see what your home could become? Browse our kitchen portfolio, explore ADU construction options, or use our remodel cost calculator to get an instant ballpark estimate. Then schedule your free consultation and let us turn your vision into reality.
